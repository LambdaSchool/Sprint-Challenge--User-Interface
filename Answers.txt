1. If you had to describe semantic HTML to the next cohort of students, what would you say?
   Semantic Html gives meaning to web pages rather then just presenting them. We could have
   a web page full of divs but how would you know what a <p> or even <h1>? That where semantic
   html comes in. 
2. Name two big differences between display: block; and display: inline;?
   Block means that the element is displayed as a block. Inline means that the 
   element is displayed inline.
