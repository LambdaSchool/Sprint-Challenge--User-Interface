2. Name two big differences between ```display: block;``` and ```display: inline;```.
    a. display: inline Can't have height or width. Forces a line break. Left and right margins, but not top and bottom margins. Will respect content to its right and left.
    b. dispaly: block. Doesn't force a line break. Allows padding and margins on all sides. Can set height and width. Will respect elements to its right and left.

3. What are the 4 areas of the box model?
    a. Content: This is where the content and images will appear.
    b. Padding: Opens an area around the content. It is transparent.
    c. Border: This goes around both the padding and the content.
    d. Margin: This opens an area outside the border. It is transparent.
