Q1. If you were to describe semantic HTML to the next cohort of students, what would you say?

I would say, semantic HTML is a markup language that provides meaning and structure to the content of the page regardless of the style.

Q2. Name two big differences between display: block; and display: inline;.

display: block: They take up full width of the page by default but the desired height and width can also be given. In addition block elements start from new line.
display: inline: They take up only the amount of space it needs. They also display side by side on the same line unlike block elements. 

Q3.What are the 4 areas of the box model?

The four areas of box-model are: the content edge, padding edge, border edge, and the margin edge.

Q4. While using flexbox, what axis does the following property work on: align-items: center?

It would work on cross-axis (means y axis on the grid).
